Welcome To Albacheese iHome

The app was developed by Josh Frampton and Ryan Dooley from the Team Formerly Known as Albacheese.

This app is meant to be used with the companion website for the full experience.

This app has the ability to fully control our virtual home's lights and alarm system. With this app you can leave the house with the knowledge that you will be safe.

By simply glancing at the app you can see the states of both the alarm and the lights and adjust them accordingly knowing that your virtual home will be maintained accordingly. This app revolutionizes the way we can percieve our virtual homes and ultimately, change our lives entirely for the better.
